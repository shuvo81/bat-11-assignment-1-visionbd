Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovation

Promoting advanced technology, startups, creativity, and transforming industries through innovation.

Sustainability

Emphasizing green energy, eco-friendly practices, and long-term environmental stewardship.

Infrastructure

Building modern, resilient infrastructure including bridges, highways, smart cities, and urbanization.

Empowerment

Enhancing education, equality, entrepreneurship, microfinance, and social upliftment.
