Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovation

Promoting advanced technology, startups, creativity, and transforming industries through innovation.

Sustainability

Emphasizing green energy, eco-friendly practices, and long-term environmental stewardship.

Infrastructure

Building modern, resilient infrastructure including bridges, highways, smart cities, and urbanization.

Empowerment

Enhancing education, equality, entrepreneurship, microfinance, and social upliftment.

The Vision of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 aims to redefine the nation’s trajectory by embracing cutting-edge technology, promoting inclusive growth, and ensuring sustainability. With initiatives in renewable energy, digital transformation, and robust infrastructure, the country is set to become a global model of development.

Digital Transformation

Creating a nationwide digital ecosystem to enhance connectivity and access to services for all citizens.

Green Energy

Investments in solar and wind energy to ensure a sustainable and eco-friendly future.

Modern Infrastructure

Building state-of-the-art infrastructure to support economic growth and global competitiveness.

"The strength of Bangladesh lies in the determination and resilience of its people. By fostering opportunities for all and embracing innovation, we are laying the foundation for a brighter, fairer, and more prosperous future. Our vision is to transform Bangladesh into a nation driven by innovation, powered by youth, and sustained by social justice. Together, we can build a resilient economy and an inclusive future where no one is left behind."

Dr. Muhammad Yunus

Chief Adviser of the People's Republic of Bangladesh
